Travel Smarter

Petrol cars emit ~0.18kg CO2 per km. Electric cars emit just 0.05kg. Learn how commuting choices add up.

Eat Green

Meat-heavy meals average 2.5kg CO2, while vegan meals represent only 0.5kg. Small swaps equal huge offsets.

🔌

Conserve Energy

Phantom standby loads represent up to 10% of utility bills. Switch to LEDs and save 0.8kg CO2 per bulb.
